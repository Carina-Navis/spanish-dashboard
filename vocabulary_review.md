# 📚 Spanish Vocabulary Review List

Review these periodically to build retention. Words are added as they come up in lessons.

---

## Verbs

| Spanish | English | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| sugerir | to suggest | Stem-changing: e→ie (*sugiero*). Triggers subjunctive: *sugerir que + subjunctive* |
| recomendar | to recommend | Stem-changing: e→ie (*recomiendo*). Triggers subjunctive. |
| proteger | to protect | Irregular yo: *protejo* → subjunctive: *proteja, protejas...* |
| lograr | to manage to / to achieve | Common in writing: *logramos terminar* = we managed to finish |

## Nouns

| Spanish | English | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| el contratiempo | setback / obstacle | From Lesson 1 reading comprehension |
| el ambiente | environment / atmosphere | Can mean surroundings or vibe depending on context |
| la bolsa | bag | Also means "stock market" in financial contexts |

## Phrases & Connectors

| Spanish | English | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| a pesar de (que) | despite / in spite of | Followed by noun (*a pesar de los problemas*) or subjunctive clause |
| a menos que | unless | Always triggers subjunctive |
| para que | so that | Always triggers subjunctive |
| sin embargo | however / nevertheless | Formal connector, common in writing |
| ojalá (que) | I hope that / if only | Always triggers subjunctive |
