Areas to Review

⚠ estar muerto — no exceptions; death is always a state, never ser
⚠ ser for personality traits in past — era listo/bueno/malo, not estaba
⚠ Gender agreement on adjectives — recurring across lessons
⚠ por for emotional cause — nervioso por, preocupado por (not sobre)
⚠ le→se rule — must swap even when le/les is pre-written
